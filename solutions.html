Frequently Asked Questions

What industries does Karnot serve?

Karnot serves manufacturing, commercial buildings, hotels, agriculture and dairy, cold chain logistics, and industrial process heating and cooling with CO2 and R290 natural refrigerant heat pumps.

What is iMESH and how fast is the payback?

iMESH is Karnot's bolt-on retrofit for existing chillers that delivers 20-30% efficiency improvement without replacing equipment. Most sites achieve payback in approximately 21 weeks.

What is Hydrogreen vertical fodder?

Hydrogreen is a climate-controlled vertical fodder production system that converts 1 kg of seed into 6-8 kg of fresh fodder in 6-7 days, using 95% less water than field-grown feed. When paired with Karnot heat recovery, it becomes energy-neutral.
