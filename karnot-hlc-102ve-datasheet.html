iZONE Vertical Exposed & Floor Standing Fan Coil Units

Freestanding tower fan coil units for hydronic heating and cooling distribution. The HLC VE series offers 9 models in a slim 270 mm wide profile for standard rooms and retrofit installations. The HLC-LZ series provides high-capacity floor standing units for commercial applications requiring up to 15 kW of heating output.

All units connect to central iHEAT heat pumps or chillers via 2-pipe water circuits, delivering zone-controlled comfort without refrigerant in occupied spaces.

Standard Test Conditions

Rated Capacity Conditions

  • Cooling: Chilled water inlet/outlet 7/12 °C, room air dry-bulb/wet-bulb 27/19.5 °C
  • Heating: Hot water inlet/outlet 60/50 °C, room air dry-bulb 20 °C
  • Noise: Measured at 1.5 m distance in semi-anechoic conditions, high fan speed
  • Water flow: Based on rated capacity at standard temperature differentials

High Static Pressure Configurations

For installations requiring ducted air distribution from the tower unit, high static pressure variants are available on the HLC 102VE and larger models. These configurations use upgraded fan assemblies to overcome duct resistance while maintaining rated airflow. Contact Karnot technical support for high static pressure model selection and duct design guidance.

Installation & Applications

Hotel Rooms & Suites

Slim 270 mm profile fits against walls or in alcoves. Quiet operation from 37 dB(A) ensures guest comfort. Individual zone control per room.

Offices & Meeting Rooms

Freestanding installation requires no ceiling work. Three-speed fan control adapts to occupancy. Models from 1.8 kW to 12.6 kW cover individual offices to open-plan floors.

Retail & Showrooms

Tower design complements interior layouts. HLC-LZ floor standing units deliver up to 15 kW heating for large ground-floor retail spaces with high ceilings.

Residential Retrofit

Replaces radiators with heating and cooling capability. No ceiling void required. Connects to iHEAT heat pump for a complete PFAS-free hydronic system.

Healthcare & Care Homes

Water-based distribution eliminates airborne refrigerant risk. Easy-access filters for hygiene maintenance. Quiet low-speed operation for patient areas.

Easy Retrofit Installation

No false ceiling required. Simple 2-pipe water connection. Electrical connection to standard supply. Floor-standing units can be operational within hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between the HLC VE and HLC-LZ series?

The HLC VE series (34VE-238VE) are slim 270 mm wide vertical exposed tower units designed for standard rooms and retrofit installations. The HLC-LZ series are larger floor standing high-capacity units (3HP and 5HP) for commercial spaces requiring 7.4-15 kW of cooling capacity.

Can the tower fan coil units provide both heating and cooling?

Yes. All models operate as 2-pipe changeover units, providing chilled water cooling in summer and hot water heating in winter when connected to an iHEAT heat pump or chiller. The HLC 102VE, for example, delivers 5.4 kW cooling and 8.1 kW heating.

What are the standard test conditions for the rated capacities?

Cooling: inlet water 7/12 C, room DB/WB 27/19.5 C. Heating: inlet water 60/50 C, room DB 20 C. Actual performance may vary with different water temperatures and ambient conditions.

How quiet are the vertical exposed fan coil units?

Noise levels range from 37 dB(A) on the smallest HLC 34VE to 52 dB(A) on the largest HLC 238VE at high fan speed. Lower fan speeds reduce noise significantly. The 37 dB(A) level is comparable to a whisper.

What pipe connections do these fan coil units use?

The HLC VE series uses standard 2-pipe water connections for supply and return. The units are designed for hydronic systems and connect to central heat pumps or chillers via insulated copper or plastic pipework.
