Karnot Permafrost P5 Thermal Battery

Low-temperature cooling storage using Plentigrade P5 phase-change material

The Karnot Permafrost P5 delivers highly efficient, low-temperature cooling storage for commercial and industrial applications. Using Plentigrade P5 PCM, it provides on-demand cooling down to −40°C in a compact, modular form factor. Ideal for cold chain logistics, process cooling, and demand-side management of refrigeration loads.

38 kWh Capacity Rapid Cooling Dual-Circuit Plentigrade PCM

Key Features & Advantages

Engineered for ultra-low-temperature performance and operational flexibility — the Permafrost P5 delivers powerful cooling storage with exceptional efficiency.

Low Temp Operation (−40°C)

Charges at temperatures as low as −40°C, enabling deep-cold storage for demanding refrigeration and process cooling applications.

High-Performance Circuits

Features two independent hydronic circuits (High Power and Low Power) that can be configured to suit any cooling application and load requirement.

Rapid Response (50 L/min)

High flow rates of up to 50 L/min on the HP circuit allow the battery to respond rapidly to peak cooling demands and recharge quickly.

Exceptional Efficiency

Industry-leading standby energy loss of just 0.91 kWh per 24 hours ensures stored cooling energy is retained for when it is needed most.

Additional Advantages

Beyond core performance, the Permafrost P5 delivers system-wide benefits that transform how you manage cooling energy.

System Benefits

  • Decouples cooling generation from cooling demand for maximum operational flexibility
  • Enables demand side management and shifts cooling loads to cheaper off-peak electricity tariffs
  • Modular and scalable — thermal batteries can be connected in series or in parallel to increase cooling storage capacity
  • Reduces peak electrical demand by storing cooling energy during low-demand periods
  • Integrates with existing chillers, heat pumps, and HVAC&R equipment
  • Supports cold chain resilience with stored cooling reserve for backup during power interruptions

Lower Costs

A Karnot Permafrost P5 thermal battery is clean and fast to install. Running costs are low because the exceptional 0.91 kWh/day standby loss minimises energy waste, and mandatory annual maintenance and inspections are not required.
