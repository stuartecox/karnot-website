Frequently Asked Questions

Is the AquaHERO 200L eligible for the UK Boiler Upgrade Scheme?

Yes. The AquaHERO 200L is MCS certified, making it eligible for the £7,500 Boiler Upgrade Scheme (BUS) grant when installed by an MCS-registered installer.

What is the COP of the AquaHERO 200L?

The AquaHERO 200L has a COP of 4.0 at rated conditions (20°C ambient, water heated from 9°C to 55°C). This means it delivers 4 kWh of heat for every 1 kWh of electricity consumed — approximately 75% cheaper than direct electric resistance heating.

Can the AquaHERO 200L operate in cold weather?

Yes. The AquaHERO 200L operates in ambient temperatures from -7°C to 43°C. At -7°C it delivers 1.24 kW heating capacity. A 1.5 kW electric backup heater provides top-up heating at very low temperatures.
