What is iMESH?

A revolutionary adiabatic pre-cooling system that dramatically improves chiller efficiency through intelligent heat rejection optimisation

iMESH pre-cools incoming air to your chiller's condenser using evaporative cooling technology. By lowering the ambient temperature before it reaches your equipment, we reduce the temperature difference your chiller needs to overcome — dramatically cutting compressor workload and energy consumption.
