Key Features & Advantages

Engineered for simplicity, reliability, and performance — the iHEAT Hydronic Tank streamlines your installation while delivering superior thermal management.

High Functional Integration

Combines a 150L hot water tank, 60L buffer tank, expansion tank, and 3-way valve. Includes advanced logic for solar heating and return water control.

Dual Temperature Zone Control

Supports precise control of two separate temperature zones (e.g., floor heating and radiators), ensuring optimal comfort without extra hardware.

Simplified Installation

A segmented design with highly integrated circuit boards and terminal accessories dramatically reduces installation time by over 50%, reducing complexity and potential errors.

Triple Safety Protection

Features dual over-temperature protection, a safety pressure relief valve, and automatic pressure reset functions for stable, reliable operation.
