Frequently Asked Questions

What engineering tools are available in the Karnot Engineering Hub?

The Karnot Engineering Hub provides free calculators including electrical cable sizing (BS 7671/IEC 60364), imperial-to-metric unit conversion, mass flow rate and pump sizing, cooling tower evaluation, refrigerant oil charge calculation, thread dimension reference, and cold room load estimation.

Are these engineering calculators free to use?

Yes, all calculators in the Karnot Engineering Hub are completely free with no login required. They are built by our engineering team to help HVAC, refrigeration, and mechanical professionals with daily calculations.

What standards do the calculators follow?

Our calculators follow international standards including BS 7671 (IET Wiring Regulations), IEC 60364, and use industry-standard lookup tables for cable ratings, derating factors, and thread dimensions from DIN, ISO, BSP, NPT, JIS, and ANSI specifications.

Can I use these calculators for heat pump installations?

Absolutely. The cable sizing calculator includes quick-select buttons for common Karnot heat pump power ratings (9.5kW to 100kW), and the unit converter covers all flow, pressure, energy, and temperature conversions needed for heat pump system design.
