Karnot iSTOR P89 High-Temp Thermal Battery

High-temperature thermal storage for industrial processes and commercial heating

The Karnot iSTOR P89 delivers industrial-grade high-temperature thermal storage using advanced Plentigrade P89 Phase Change Material. With charging temperatures up to 120°C and a discharge temperature of 83.5°C, it is purpose-built for demanding process heat, district heating, and high-temperature commercial applications where conventional thermal stores fall short.

67 kWh Capacity High-Temp Output Dual-Circuit Plentigrade PCM

Key Features & Advantages

Engineered for high-temperature industrial applications — the iSTOR P89 delivers powerful thermal storage with exceptional heat output in a compact, robust package.

Industrial-Grade Heat

Charges up to 120°C and discharges at 83.5°C, delivering the high-temperature output required for industrial processes and demanding commercial heating applications.

Dense Energy Storage

Packs 67 kWh of thermal storage into a compact footprint using advanced Plentigrade P89 Phase Change Material for maximum energy density.

Versatile Dual Circuits

Features two independent hydronic circuits (High Power and Low Power) that can be configured to suit any application and load requirement.

Robust & Reliable

Built to withstand demanding environments with a maximum working pressure of 16 bar, ensuring long-term reliability in industrial settings.

Additional Advantages

Beyond core performance, the iSTOR P89 delivers system-wide benefits that transform how you manage high-temperature thermal energy.

System Benefits

  • Charges from multiple energy and heat sources
  • Decouples heat sources from heating demands
  • Offers demand side management and shifts heating loads to cheaper off-peak tariffs
  • Modular and scalable — thermal batteries can be connected in series or in parallel to increase heat storage capacity

Lower Costs

A Karnot iSTOR P89 thermal battery is clean and fast to install. There's no need to increase the size of expansion vessels nor the volume of water treatment chemicals. Running costs are also low because mandatory annual maintenance and inspections are not required.
