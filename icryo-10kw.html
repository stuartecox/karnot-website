Advanced Air-Cycle Technology

Open-Cycle Design

The system operates on the principle of an open-air cycle: air passes through an integrated snow trap, is cooled by the turbo module, and is then delivered to the client's chamber at the specified temperature. This ensures reliable and stable performance even at extremely low temperatures.

Zero Environmental Impact

By using natural air (R729) as the refrigerant, the iCRYO 10 achieves zero Global Warming Potential (GWP), zero Ozone Depletion Potential (ODP), and produces no TFA (trifluoroacetic acid). Air is free, abundant, and requires no specialised handling or disposal.

Oil-Free Turbo-Compressor

The advanced turbo-compressor design operates completely oil-free, eliminating contamination risks critical for pharmaceutical, semiconductor, and food applications. This also dramatically reduces maintenance costs and extends component lifespan.

Snow Catcher System

Humidity Extraction Device (HED)

At ultra-low temperatures, moisture in the air instantly converts to snow. The integrated Snow Catcher — a Humidity Extraction Device — prevents frost buildup on products and equipment, eliminating the need for energy-consuming defrost cycles.

Continuous Operation

Unlike traditional cryogenic systems that require periodic defrosting (causing temperature fluctuations and product quality concerns), the iCRYO 10 operates continuously without interruption, maintaining stable temperatures and ensuring consistent process conditions.

Control & Safety Features

Intelligent Load Management

Partial load operation capability ensures high energy efficiency by matching cooling output to actual demand, reducing unnecessary power consumption during low-demand periods.

Rapid Recovery

In the event of power interruption, the system automatically restarts and restores all operational parameters within 30 seconds, minimising disruption to critical processes and protecting sensitive materials.

Frequently Asked Questions

What refrigerant does the iCRYO 10 use?

The iCRYO 10 uses R729 — natural air — as its refrigerant. This gives it zero GWP, zero ODP, and produces no TFA. Air is free, abundant, and requires no specialist handling or disposal.

What temperature range does the iCRYO 10 achieve?

The iCRYO 10 operates from -40°C to -110°C with stable, reliable performance. Cooling capacity is 5.65 kW at -40°C, 4.37 kW at -70°C, and 2.49 kW at -110°C.

Does the iCRYO 10 need defrost cycles?

No. The integrated Snow Catcher (Humidity Extraction Device) continuously removes moisture before it can cause frost buildup, enabling uninterrupted operation without defrost cycles.
