Frequently Asked Questions

What applications is the Karnot iHEAT 25kW suitable for?

The 25kW is designed for light commercial buildings, larger HMOs, small offices, schools, and multi-unit developments. Its 75°C output works with existing radiator systems and supports domestic hot water production alongside space heating and cooling.

What makes the 25kW different from the residential 18.5kW?

The 25kW uses twin HIGHLY DC inverter rotary compressors for greater redundancy and efficiency at part-load. It requires 380-420V three-phase supply, has a larger refrigerant charge (1.80kg R290), and uses a CHICO 4.3-inch touchscreen controller rather than the Carel controller on residential units.

Does the 25kW iHEAT use R290 refrigerant?

Yes. The CGK-080V4-B uses R290 (propane) with a charge of 1.80kg. R290 has a GWP of 3 — 675x lower than R410A — and is fully compliant with EU F-gas regulations and future PFAS restrictions.

What cooling output does the 25kW heat pump provide?

The 25kW delivers up to 20.1kW of cooling (EER 3.23-3.75 at A35/W18 conditions). When paired with iZONE fan coil units, it provides a complete commercial heating, hot water, and cooling solution.

What is the sound level of the 25kW commercial heat pump?

The CGK-080V4-B operates at 63 dBA at 1 metre — suitable for commercial plant rooms and installations where some separation from occupied areas is provided. Planning applications should confirm local noise limits.
