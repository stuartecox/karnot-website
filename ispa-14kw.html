iSPA 14R

14kW R32 Inverter Pool Temperature Control System

The Karnot iSPA 14R delivers precise pool temperature control — both heating and cooling — with ultra-low GWP R32 refrigerant and DC inverter technology. Featuring a corrosion-proof titanium-in-PVC heat exchanger, Panasonic twin rotary compressor, and built-in Wi-Fi control, it provides year-round comfort for swimming pools from 30 to 60 cubic metres.

Frequently Asked Questions

What pool size is the iSPA 14R recommended for?

The iSPA 14R is recommended for swimming pools between 30 and 60 cubic metres. It delivers 14kW nominal heating capacity with a COP up to 14.82 under optimal conditions.

Can the iSPA 14R both heat and cool a pool?

Yes. The iSPA 14R provides both heating (up to 14kW) and cooling (up to 7.7kW) for year-round pool temperature management.

What refrigerant does the iSPA 14R use?

The iSPA 14R uses R32 refrigerant, which has an ultra-low global warming potential (GWP of 675) — approximately 68% lower than R410A. Combined with DC inverter technology, it maximises energy efficiency.
