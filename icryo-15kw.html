Advanced Air-Cycle Technology

Open-Cycle Design

The system operates on the principle of an open-air cycle: air passes through an integrated snow trap, is cooled by the turbo module, and is then delivered to the client's chamber at the specified temperature. This ensures reliable and stable performance even at extremely low temperatures.

Enhanced Cooling Capacity

With a robust 15kW motor, the iCRYO 15 provides ample cooling for mid-scale facilities — biological and pharma storage banks, cryotherapy chambers, and industrial metal treatment. Partial load operation ensures high energy efficiency and flexibility to meet specific application requirements.

Zero Environmental Impact

Using natural air (R729) as the sole refrigerant delivers zero GWP, zero ODP, and zero TFA. Air is free, abundant, and requires no specialised handling, storage, or disposal — ever.

Oil-Free Air-Cooled Compressor

No Water Cooling Required

The iCRYO 15 features an air-cooled turbo-compressor, eliminating the need for chilled water infrastructure. This simplifies installation and reduces operational complexity compared to water-cooled models.

Contamination-Free Operation

Completely oil-free operation eliminates contamination risks critical for pharmaceutical, semiconductor, and food-grade applications — while dramatically reducing maintenance costs and extending component lifespan.

Snow Catcher System

Humidity Extraction Device (HED)

At ultra-low temperatures, moisture in the air instantly converts to snow. The integrated Snow Catcher removes this moisture continuously, preventing frost buildup on products and equipment and eliminating defrost cycles entirely.

Continuous Operation

Unlike traditional cryogenic systems that require periodic defrosting — causing temperature fluctuations and product quality concerns — the iCRYO 15 runs without interruption, maintaining stable conditions and consistent process performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the iCRYO 15 differ from the iCRYO 10?

The iCRYO 15 has a 15kW motor (vs 10.5kW) delivering 9.58 kW cooling at -40°C (vs 5.65 kW). It uses an air-cooled turbo-compressor rather than water-cooled, simplifying installation. It's suited for mid-scale facilities like cryotherapy chambers, biological storage banks, and industrial metal treatment.

Does the iCRYO 15 require water cooling?

No. The iCRYO 15 uses an air-cooled turbo-compressor, eliminating the need for chilled water infrastructure. This simplifies installation and reduces operational complexity compared to water-cooled models.

What is the Snow Catcher system on the iCRYO 15?

The Snow Catcher is an integrated Humidity Extraction Device (HED) that removes moisture from the air before it reaches ultra-low temperatures, preventing frost buildup. This enables continuous operation without defrost cycles, maintaining stable process temperatures.
