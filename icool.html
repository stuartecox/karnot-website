Frequently Asked Questions

Why is CO2 better than HFC refrigerants?

CO2 (R744) has a GWP of just 1 compared to 1,430-2,346 for common HFCs. It is non-toxic, non-flammable, PFAS-free, abundant and inexpensive. CO2 systems deliver up to 50% energy savings and are completely exempt from Kigali Amendment phase-downs.

What temperatures can iCOOL achieve?

iCOOL systems cover medium temperature (0 C to -10 C) for chilled storage and low temperature (down to -40 C) for frozen storage. Multi-temperature configurations available in a single system.

Is iCOOL suitable for the Philippines?

Yes. Transcritical CO2 systems are specifically engineered for high ambient temperatures. The Panasonic 2-stage compressor maintains efficiency even at 45 C ambient, making it ideal for tropical climates.
