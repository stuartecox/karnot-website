iSPA 28N

28kW R32 Inverter Pool Temperature Control System

The Karnot iSPA 28N is the flagship model in the iSPA range, delivering powerful pool temperature control — both heating and cooling — with ultra-low GWP R32 refrigerant and DC inverter technology. Featuring a corrosion-proof titanium-in-PVC heat exchanger, Panasonic twin rotary compressor, and built-in Wi-Fi control, it provides year-round comfort for larger commercial and residential pools from 50 to 100 cubic metres.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum heating capacity of the iSPA 28N?

The iSPA 28N delivers 28kW nominal heating capacity with a heating range from 8.68 to 28kW. Under optimal conditions (Condition 1), it achieves a COP up to 14.10.

Is the iSPA 28N suitable for large commercial pools?

Yes. The iSPA 28N is the largest model in the iSPA range, recommended for pools between 50 and 100 cubic metres. It is ideal for commercial pools, resorts, hotels, and large residential installations.

Does the iSPA 28N provide pool cooling as well as heating?

Yes. The iSPA 28N provides both heating (up to 28kW) and active cooling (up to 15.4kW with an EER of 3.18), allowing year-round pool temperature management.
