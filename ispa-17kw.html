iSPA 17R

17kW R32 Inverter Pool Temperature Control System

The Karnot iSPA 17R delivers precise pool temperature control — both heating and cooling — with ultra-low GWP R32 refrigerant and DC inverter technology. Featuring a corrosion-proof titanium-in-PVC heat exchanger, Panasonic twin rotary compressor, and built-in Wi-Fi control, it provides year-round comfort for swimming pools from 40 to 80 cubic metres.

CE Certified R32 Low GWP DC Inverter Titanium HX

Key Features at a Glance

R32 Inverter

Ultra-low GWP R32 refrigerant with DC inverter technology for maximum efficiency.

Titanium Heat Exchanger

Corrosion-proof titanium-in-PVC for pool and saltwater durability.

Panasonic Compressor

Twin rotary Panasonic compressor for quiet, reliable operation.

Wi-Fi Smart Control

Built-in Wi-Fi controller for remote monitoring and scheduling.

Wide Operating Range

Operates from -15°C to 45°C ambient for year-round pool temperature control — heating in winter, cooling in summer.

Quiet Operation

DC inverter fan with low noise for residential installations.

Ideal Applications

The iSPA 17R is engineered for precise pool temperature control — keeping your pool comfortable whether it's too cold or too hot — across a wide range of aquatic installations.

Maintain the perfect swimming temperature year-round — heat when it's cold, cool when tropical sun overheats your pool.
