Are Karnot R290 heat pumps eligible for the Boiler Upgrade Scheme (BUS) grant?

Yes. Karnot iHEAT R290 air source heat pumps are eligible for the £7,500 Boiler Upgrade Scheme grant in England and Wales when installed by an MCS-certified installer. Homeowners can additionally claim a £2,500 cooling grant, bringing total available grant funding to £10,000 per household. Karnot provides full MCS compliance documentation with every unit.

Every Karnot iHEAT R290 unit includes a free MCS compliance pack with all required technical documentation, saving installers £1,200–£1,600 per job compared to umbrella scheme fees. Packs include MCS product certification data, technical specifications, performance test reports, installation guidelines, and commissioning checklists aligned with MCS 020 and MCS 007 standards.
