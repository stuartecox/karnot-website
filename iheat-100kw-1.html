Frequently Asked Questions

What applications is the Karnot iHEAT 100kW designed for?

The 100kW is designed for large commercial and light industrial applications: large hotels, hospitals, food processing facilities, industrial process heating, and district heating schemes. With 87.5kW simultaneous cooling capacity, it handles year-round thermal loads. Multiple units can be cascaded with modular controls.

Does the CGK-300V4-B have redundancy?

Yes. The CGK-300V4-B uses dual independent HITACHI Inverter Scroll compressor circuits. If one circuit requires maintenance, the other continues operating at reduced capacity — critical for applications where thermal supply continuity is essential.

What is the R290 charge on the 100kW unit?

The CGK-300V4-B holds 9.4kg of R290 across two independent 4.7kg circuits. R290 (propane) has a GWP of 3, is PFAS-free, and is classified as A3 flammable — the unit is designed with safe-charge engineering to meet EN 378 and IEC 60335-2-40 requirements.
