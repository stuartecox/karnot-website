iCRYO 20

Advanced Air-Cycle Technology

Open-Cycle Design

The system operates on the principle of an open-air cycle: air passes through an integrated snow trap, is cooled by the turbo module, and is delivered to the client's chamber at the specified temperature — ensuring reliable, stable performance even at the most extreme temperatures.

High-Capacity Performance

With a 22kW motor delivering 13.7 kW at -40°C, the iCRYO 20 is built for larger-scale industrial applications. Partial load operation ensures high energy efficiency and flexibility to match actual cooling demand without unnecessary energy consumption.

Zero Environmental Impact

Using natural air (R729) as the sole refrigerant — zero GWP, zero ODP, zero TFA. Air is free, abundant, and requires no specialised handling, storage, or disposal under any current or future regulation.

Water-Cooled Turbo-Compressor

Superior Heat Rejection

The water-cooled turbo-compressor provides more efficient heat removal than air cooling — particularly important at higher capacities and in continuous industrial operation where ambient temperatures may be elevated.

Contamination-Free Operation

Completely oil-free operation eliminates contamination risks critical for food, pharmaceutical, and semiconductor applications. Maintenance costs are dramatically reduced and component lifespan extended compared to conventional compressors.

Snow Catcher System

Humidity Extraction Device (HED)

At ultra-low temperatures, airborne moisture instantly converts to snow. The integrated Snow Catcher continuously removes this moisture, preventing frost buildup on products and equipment and eliminating defrost cycles entirely.

Continuous Industrial Operation

The iCRYO 20 runs without interruption — no defrost downtime, no temperature fluctuations — making it ideal for continuous industrial processes and critical pharmaceutical operations where product integrity cannot be compromised.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the iCRYO 20 differ from the iCRYO 15?

The iCRYO 20 has a 22kW motor delivering 13.7 kW cooling at -40°C, compared to the iCRYO 15's 15kW motor and 9.58 kW capacity. It returns to water-cooled operation for superior heat rejection in demanding industrial environments, and is suited for larger-scale applications including industrial food storage, pharmaceutical manufacturing, and advanced materials testing.

Why does the iCRYO 20 use water cooling?

Water cooling provides more efficient heat rejection than air cooling — particularly important at higher capacities and in continuous industrial operation. It enables the iCRYO 20 to sustain peak performance in demanding environments where ambient temperatures may be elevated.

Is the iCRYO 20 suitable for food storage applications?

Yes. The iCRYO 20 uses natural air (R729) with zero GWP and is 100% oil-free, making it ideal for food applications where refrigerant leaks or oil contamination are unacceptable. Its continuous operation without defrost cycles maintains stable temperatures critical for food quality.
