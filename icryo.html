iCRYO

Ultra-Low Temperature CryoChillers for Advanced Industrial Applications

Seamless Integration, Continuous Operation

Karnot's iCRYO systems deliver consistent ultra-low temperature control — without complex defrosting cycles or extra in-room equipment. The open-cycle air refrigeration system supplies air cooled by a turbo module at your specified temperature.

Operating Range: -40 °C to -130 °C

Minimal Maintenance: No refrigerant leakage tests, no refrigerant purchase costs, no replenishment. Air is supplied free of charge. Oil-free operation ensures long service life.

The Snow Catcher Advantage

At extremely low temperatures where moisture instantly turns to snow, the unique Snow Catcher — a Humidity Extraction Device (HED) — prevents frost buildup on your products. This eliminates energy-consuming defrosting cycles, allowing continuous, efficient operation.

Installation is simple — just a few wall openings connect the iCRYO unit and the Snow Catcher, making setup as effortless as the reliability it provides.

Advanced Applications

Ultra-low temperature solutions for high-tech industries

Semiconductor Manufacturing

Cold temperatures evaluate device performance under extreme conditions, revealing defects affecting electron mobility. Dry etching uses cold temps for precision surface shaping.

Freeze-Drying (Lyophilisation)

Sophisticated dehydration preserving structural integrity and bioactivity. Essential for vaccines, antibiotics, and specialty foods.

Chemical Processes

Cold temperatures control reaction rates, prevent runaway exothermic reactions, and achieve selectivity and purity in products.

Physical Vapour Deposition

Enhanced PVD at -140 °C to +50 °C, accelerating vacuum processes. iCRYO integrates easily with existing PVD systems.

Space Equipment Testing

Simulating harsh space conditions to ensure materials and components withstand freezing without failure.

Freeze Chambers

Preserving biological samples, testing material durability, and flash-freezing food to retain freshness and quality.

Cryotherapy

Muscle pain relief, reduced swelling, and sports recovery. Exposure to -100 °C to -160 °C dry air for 2-4 minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What refrigerant does iCRYO use?

iCRYO uses air as its refrigerant — a reverse Brayton cycle. There are zero refrigerant costs, zero leakage tests, and zero environmental impact. Air is supplied free of charge.

What is the Snow Catcher?

The Snow Catcher is a patented Humidity Extraction Device (HED) that prevents frost buildup on products at ultra-low temperatures. It eliminates energy-consuming defrost cycles, allowing continuous operation.

What industries use iCRYO?

iCRYO serves semiconductor manufacturing, pharmaceutical freeze-drying (lyophilisation), chemical processes, PVD coating, space equipment testing, freeze chambers, cryotherapy, and LNG gas liquefaction.
