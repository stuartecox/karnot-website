Frequently Asked Questions

What smart home platforms does iSAVE support?

iSAVE is Matter-certified and works with Apple Home, Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and Samsung SmartThings. Thread mesh networking provides reliable, low-power wireless control with offline capability.

How do iSAVE carbon credits work?

iSAVE continuously monitors energy savings vs a Gold Standard-approved baseline. Avoided CO2 emissions are calculated, verified via blockchain, and issued as tradeable Gold Standard carbon credits. Typical returns: 8-15% of energy savings annually.

What is Virtual Power Plant (VPP) integration?

VPP allows your heat pump to participate in grid demand response programs, earning payments for shifting or curtailing energy use during grid stress events. iSAVE connects to platforms like Octopus Energy Kraken and Enel X.
