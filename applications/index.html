Frequently Asked Questions

What industries does Karnot serve?

Karnot serves hotels and hospitality, food processing and manufacturing, hospitals and healthcare, dairy and agriculture, cold chain and logistics, and data centres. Every solution uses natural refrigerants (R290 or CO₂) and is engineered for the Philippine tropical climate.

Do Karnot solutions work in the Philippine climate?

Yes. R290 and CO₂ heat pumps actually perform better in tropical climates. Higher ambient temperatures of 28-35°C improve COP ratings, making the Philippines an ideal market for heat pump efficiency. Our iHEAT R290 achieves COP 4.0-5.0 year-round.

What savings can Philippine businesses expect?

Savings range from 60-78% on thermal energy costs depending on your industry. Hotels save over ₱626,000 per year, food processors over ₱1.4 million, and hospitals over ₱1 million compared to diesel or LPG boilers at current fuel prices.

Are these solutions compliant with SEC PFRS S2?

Yes. All Karnot solutions use natural refrigerants with GWP of 1-3, eliminating Scope 1 refrigerant emissions. This directly supports Philippine SEC PFRS S2 climate disclosure requirements for listed companies and aligns with the Kigali Amendment HFC phase-down schedule.
